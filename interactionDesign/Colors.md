# Colors

# Colour models

There are a few basic colour models to be aware of. Three of these are: RGB, CMYK and HSLA.

## HSLA

*   Hue
*   Saturation
*   Lightness
*   Alpha

## CMYK

*   C: Cyan
*   M: Magenta
*   Y: Yellow
*   K: Key(Black)

## RGB

*   R: Red
*   G: Green
*   B: Blue
